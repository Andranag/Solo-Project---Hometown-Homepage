Visit Arta

A historic city in northwestern Greece

Top three activities to do in Arta

  • Visit the Bridge of Arta, a historic stone bridge built in the 17th century.
  • Explore the Paralimnio Park, a beautiful park with a lake and walking paths.
  • Stroll down Skoufas Street, a lively street with shops, cafes, and restaurants.
